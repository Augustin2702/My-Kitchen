Zucchini risotto and prosciuto

A succulent risotto ready in 33 minutes top time !

Add to cart

Prep Time

13 minutes

Per serving

609 kcal

Cook Time

20 minutes

Recipe

Step 1

Make a broth by boiling 1 cup of water (per person) with one piece of bouillon cube (make sure it dissolves completely).

Step 2

Wash 3 zucchinis and grate them. Then, peel and chop the shallot.

Step 3

In a new pot, brown the chopped shallot with a pat of butter for one minute, then add 350g of uncooked rice. Add 10cl of white wine and let it cook until the rice absorbs the buttery liquid.

Step 4

Now add zucchinis and one ladle of your broth to the pot with your rice. Cook on medium until the rice absorbs all the broth. Keep adding your broth until the rice has absorbed it all (at which point it will be done). This should take about 18-20 minutes.

Step 5

If your rice isn't the right consistency in about 20 minutes, add some water and continue cooking until it is. Then, add the parmesan.

Step 6

Scoop the risotto into a bowl with 5 slides of prosciutto, salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
